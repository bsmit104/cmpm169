Experiment 4 - ProcGen/Infinite Worlds

Description

What are you trying to acomplish with this experiment?
I call this my infinite spacetime generator. It generates an infinite space scene of planets, stars, and more. There are red, blue, green, yellow, and pink small planets that spawn randomly, big orange and red planets, and UFOs that spawn occasionally.


Technical

Describe in detail what you did technically and how it works?
stars are generated in random quantities with a hashmap. The UFOs are generated with a hashmap and use the millis() function to tween up and down in their respective tiles. The orange and red suns use a millis() function to rotate a gradient effect around the planet and are also placed using a hash map. Sometimes the smaller planets have a chance of spawning with a ring. The stars use a hashmap as well and generate a random amount of circles of various sizes within each tile.


Reflection


I think I resonate most with this generator as an artist with a reflection of my character in it. The way the stars look is genuinely awesome and I think the color variation in planets and how they spawn together is very engaging. I like when they overlap too and there really is a lot of room for interesting finds as the user explores the map.

VARIATION IN KEY Different
quantities of stars with hashmaps and different planet spawning with various quantities, clusters, and colors all help to create a lot of variation in the world. There is also the chance of UFOs spawning and even the occasional ring around the planet. These smaller variations might not be immediately seen and thus take some time to really look deeper into the space generator and spend some time exploring infinite space time.
