Experiment 2 - ProcGen/Living Impressions

Description

The goal of this project was to generate an geometric interpretation of something similar to an image. This project was done for CMPM147 Generative Design at UCSC. I chose a cyber punk futuristic city and capitalized heavily on the colors to create the similar environment, alongside building shapes and a water reflection.

Technical

I started off in Glitch with a fork of Adam Smith's interpretation of a field with trees. I used p5.js to incorporate shapes to make the city feeling.

Reflection

I worked alone on this project. I did not run into many hiccups aside from confusion surrounding how to properly get shapes in p5.js but with some help of chatgpt and Adam's started code, I was able to figure it out.
